The Best Movies According to Natalia

My top 3 movies of all-time.

I am Sam.

This is one of the most touching films that I have in my repertoire. It's the story of a mentally handicapped man fighting for custody of his seven-year-old daughter. I love the story and how this man has a great capacity to love despite his mental condition.

Divergent

This is a pretty interesting sci-fi movie, about a world divided by factions based on virtues, pretty close to reality I'd say.

The Platform

Thriller and sci-fi movie, an exploration of the importance of individual initiative in driving political change that critiques both capitalism and socialism.
